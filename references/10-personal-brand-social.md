# Personal Brand & Social Content

Use this reference for personal posts, thought leadership, founder content, carousel ideas, and social growth content.

## Personal Brand Post Types

```txt
1. Real experience
2. Lesson learned
3. Contrarian view
4. Agreement plus sharper angle
5. Mistake story
6. Process sharing
7. Case analysis
8. Notes from a conversation, project, or campaign
```

## Personal Post Formula

```txt
Real observation
Real story or situation
Insight
Lesson
Connection to work/product
Soft CTA
```

## Social Pillars

```txt
Market education
Experience sharing
Case study / proof
Personal point of view
Soft selling
Community / comment response
```

## Voice Rules

```txt
Sound like a real person with a clear point of view.
Use specific situations and examples.
Avoid vague motivational writing.
Do not force hard selling into trust-building content.
```
