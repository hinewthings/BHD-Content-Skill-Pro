# Content Frameworks

Use a framework before drafting. Do not pick randomly; select based on awareness stage, format, and goal.

## Framework Library

```txt
AIDA: Attention, Interest, Desire, Action. Good for ads and landing sections.
PAS: Problem, Agitate, Solution. Good for painful problems and sales posts.
BAB: Before, After, Bridge. Good for transformation and case studies.
4P: Promise, Picture, Proof, Push. Good for conversion ads.
FAB: Feature, Advantage, Benefit. Good for product explanations.
QUEST: Qualify, Understand, Educate, Stimulate, Transition. Good for long-form sales pages.
ACCA: Awareness, Comprehension, Conviction, Action. Good for education.
SLAP: Stop, Look, Act, Purchase. Good for short ads.
PASTOR: Problem, Amplify, Story, Transformation, Offer, Response. Good for long-form copy.
Star-Story-Solution: character, story, solution. Good for personal posts and video scripts.
Before-After-Proof: before, after, evidence. Good for case studies.
Hook-Value-CTA: direct social content.
Problem-Mistake-Fix: educational content.
Myth-Bust-Truth: thought leadership and contrarian posts.
Listicle: scannable carousels and social posts.
FAQ Selling: answer objections and transition to CTA.
```

## Selection Guide

```txt
Unaware:
ACCA, Myth-Bust-Truth, Problem-Mistake-Fix, Story.

Problem Aware:
PAS, Problem-Mistake-Fix, BAB.

Solution Aware:
FAB, Comparison, FAQ Selling, ACCA.

Product Aware:
BAB, Before-After-Proof, PASTOR, FAQ Selling.

Most Aware:
AIDA, 4P, QUEST, SLAP.

Personal brand:
Star-Story-Solution, POV, Mistake Story, Hook-Value-CTA.

Landing page:
PASTOR, AIDA, QUEST, BAB.

Facebook Ads:
PAS, 4P, SLAP, AIDA, Before-After-Proof.
```
