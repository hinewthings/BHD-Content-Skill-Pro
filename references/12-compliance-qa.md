# Compliance & QA

Use this reference before finalizing ads, landing pages, sales copy, performance claims, testimonials, and content using customer proof. For Facebook, TikTok, Vietnam advertising-law, superlative claims, or regulated product checks, load `13-platform-vietnam-compliance.md` first.

## Compliance Checklist

Check:

```txt
1. Are any numbers fabricated?
2. Are any testimonials, reviews, press mentions, logos, or customer names fabricated?
3. Are claims too absolute?
4. Are results guaranteed without proof?
5. Are competitor comparisons unsupported?
6. Are user attributes framed in a sensitive or accusatory way?
7. Are there platform ad-policy risks?
8. Is there evidence for each major claim?
9. Is copyrighted or restricted content being reused improperly?
10. Are disclaimers or assumptions needed?
11. Is this intended for Facebook, TikTok, or paid social distribution?
12. Are there unsupported superlative/absolute terms such as "nhất", "duy nhất", "tốt nhất", "số một", "number one", "best", or "only"?
13. Does the landing page match the ad claim, offer, price, and disclaimer?
14. Are AI-generated people, voices, endorsements, or edited media disclosed when needed?
15. Has proof strength been graded before using specific numbers or strong claims?
16. Has organic vs paid distribution been considered?
17. Is there exactly one primary CTA?
18. Are technical features translated into customer outcomes?
```

## Safer Claim Language

Use when proof is incomplete:

```txt
có thể giúp
được thiết kế để
mục tiêu là
phù hợp với
giúp bạn nhìn rõ hơn
theo lộ trình
trong nhiều trường hợp
```

Avoid without strong proof:

```txt
cam kết 100%
chắc chắn ra đơn
đảm bảo doanh thu
tốt nhất thị trường
không bao giờ lỗi
giúp mọi người thành công
số một
duy nhất
number one
best
only
```

## QA Score

Before final answer, evaluate:

```txt
Insight fit: /10
Journey fit: /10
Experience layer fit: /10
Hook strength: /10
Clarity: /10
Proof strength: /10
Conversion strength: /10
Brand voice fit: /10
Feature-to-outcome clarity: /10
CTA discipline: /10
Compliance risk: Low / Medium / High
Platform risk: Low / Medium / High / Not applicable
Vietnam ad-law risk: Low / Medium / High / Not applicable
```

If any score is below 7, revise before final output unless the user asked for a rough draft.
